Which two websites are you most inspired by?

shareup.com and ableton.com

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size or balance?

squareup.com

  • Size: Logo feels small. I like that things tend to be small to open up the space
  • Balance: I like it centered top with steady two column approach
  • Space: Nice that it has lots of open space and simple colors give it an open feel. It evokes a sense of ease and simplicity.

ableton.com

  • Size: I like the sizing of everything.
  • Balance: I like that it is open in the above-the-fold space, but that it focuses on creating pods of educational video content along the three columns beneath the fold
  • Space: Very nice use of open space with unique colorful type front-and-center.

What makes them similar and different?

Similar

  • Both have very simple messaging and simple, inviting font style.
  • Both mix full-bleed imagery along with content pods throughout.

Different

  • Ableton.com caters more towards people who already own the software and want education on how to use it.
